## AREAS TO AVOID

### High Crime / Safety Concerns
- **Fakulteta** - Higher crime rates
- **Filipovtsi** - Not suitable for families/expats
- **Orlandovtsi** - Poor reputation
- **Sveta Nedelya Square** - Pickpocketing zone (tourist area)

### Late Night Caution (Main Boulevards)
- Aleksandrov Boulevard
- Maria Luiza Boulevard
- Slivnitsa Boulevard
- Hristo Botev Boulevard

### Panel-Dominated (Your Criteria Excludes)
- **Lyulin** - Bulgaria's largest panel district
- **Obelya** - Heavy panel construction
- **Nadezhda** - Mostly panels
