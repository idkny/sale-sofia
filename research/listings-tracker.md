## Search Filters to Use

When searching:
- **Rooms:** 3+ (3-стаен or повече)
- **Price:** 200,000-270,000 EUR / 391,000-528,000 BGN
- **Type:** Brick/New (avoid "панел")
- **Area:** Target districts (Hadji Dimitar, Krastova Vada, Ovcha Kupel, Studentski Grad, Lozenets)
